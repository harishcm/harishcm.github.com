About this Website

This website is an integral part of my Unschooling Project, which grew from my initial forays into self-directed learning.

It seeks to provide an introduction to self-directed learning, especially for students who are currently schooling in a mainstream education system, and for parents and teachers of such students. It also acts as a showcase of my self-directed learning journeys, experiences, methods and creations.

About Me

Name: Mohanadas Harish Chandar
Location: Singapore

My passion for self-directed learning got crystallised around the year 2006. It was my first year at university, and it occurred to me that if I did not change myself, I was headed straight for the rat race. School, even with its fond memories, had not prepared me for much else. My university course was not going to either.

I was lucky. Just before entering university, I had gotten acquainted with unschooling and autodidactism. Both provided an authoritative voice that learning was a natural process. The unschooling community went further. It provided specific guidance regarding issues that students in mainstream schooling face, and what to do about it. I realised that one could apply the principles of unschooling while not leaving school as yet.

Feeling enlightened, I started keeping records of my learning pursuits and started taking my self-directed learning seriously.
