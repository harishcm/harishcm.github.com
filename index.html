Self-directed Learning

Self-directed learning is a natural process that everyone indulges in. You may have heard it by other names: unschooling, deschooling, adult education, self-learning, autodidactism and informal learning.

Since 2006, I have been trying to facilitate and improve on this natural process, and remove obstacles in its way.

My initial motivation came from realising that school had only prepared me for competing in the rat-race. If I wanted to learn about alternate ways of living and making a living, I had to do it myself.

Over time, I have been involved with several different learning pursuits and have kept several records.

This website seeks to share my experiences.
