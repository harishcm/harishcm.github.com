Learning Languages: Journal

  1. 2012-10-17
    1. Achievement
      1. Speaking Basic Mandarin Chinese at Work

          I currently work at a construction site in Singapore, and it is an environment in which there are many workers and supervisors from China who do not understand more than a few words in English. I have had a long exposure to the Chinese language since my schooling days—mainly through friends, but have largely been a passive listener with a ‘feel’ for the language.

          Now however, I finally feel comfortable with interacting and issuing instructions in Basic Chinese in my current work environment. At the same time, I want to improve my vocabulary further, so as to be able to fully understand and participate in the day to day conversations of native speakers.
