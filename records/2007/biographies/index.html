Biographies

  1. Inspiration

      It appears that many of the older adults that I meet have a lot of one liner advice for me. “Go do this”, “This is hot”, “That has future”, etc. But really I doubt such advice is really useful.

      What I do feel would be useful would be to know is how they, and other ‘common’ people, live or have lived their lives. That will allow me to learn from their way of life, experiences, successes and mistakes. I can then better formulate how I want to live my life.
