Beam Robotics

  1. Inspiration

      Edward showed me a book on building small robots from parts in ‘junk’ electronics. I got interested as it was a very affordable hobby and was based on analog electronic instead of digital.

  2. Possible Resources

      2. BY Dave Hrynkiw and Mark W. Tilden

      4. National Library Board, Singapore
    2. Sim Lim Tower, Singapore
      1. Electronics Parts Suppliers
      2. Initial Perception

          Shops in the 1st floor sell componenets such as resistors, capacitors, diodes, transistors, integrated circuits, etc. Shops in the basement sell small motors such as the B1-25 and soldering irons, etc.
