  12. About The Author

      The Author, who prefers to be called Harish, was born in 1985 in Singapore. He is now in National University of Singapore, pursuing a first degree in Civil Engineering.

      His secondary school days were spent in Victoria School and then he went on to Victoria Junior College. It was during his junior college days that he frequently thought about the meaning in schooling and encountered the dilemma of allocating of time, resources and energy to both formal and informal learning.

      During his National Service he made time to learn a little more into the fields of psychology and educational research & thought. Thereafter, he began taking his informal education more seriously and started keeping records of his learning.

      When the author was twenty years old, general elections were held in Singapore. He followed the election happenings actively and was shocked to realise that he knew next to nothing about how his country was run- even though he would be old enough to vote the following year.

      Using the surge in momentum caused by the shock and the time he had before entering his University course, he began to assemble a plausible solution to his dilemma and his lack of knowledge.

      He realised that it was unfair and unrealistic to expect schooling to provide for all learning ventures. Thus, other means of education had to be sought to compliment schooling. And much of these other means of education would have to be self ascertained and sought after.

      He hypothesized that by bringing both his formal and informal education under the same “umbrella” they could be made to compliment one another. Allocation of time, energy & resources to both could also be bettered. This in effect meant Directing his Education himself.

      Though he felt that he had a plausible solution, he also felt then that he was still not capable enough to achieve it.

      He however saw that he could take the initial steps by doing three things: * Improving his Knowledge in areas he felt were vital * Building Structure and Credibility in his informal education and * Making Self-directed Learning a well assimilated Habit in his daily life

      He grew in confidence when he realised that many of the great influential people throughout history were self-taught individuals or individuals who excelled in fields that they were never schooled in.

      The author is currently in active pursuit of the three initial steps that will he feels will enable him to direct his education himself. He has tried serious Self-directed Learning on a full-time basis in the six months before entering his University course and has experienced the sense of fulfillment and widening in internal locus of control it brings.

      His plan is influenced greatly by the methods and philosophies held by Homeschooling and Unschooling Communities and by the authors John Holt, Grace Llewllyn and Charles D. Hayes. He has however been wary to ensure that his methods and ideas are localised to his specific context and situation in Singapore.

      The author expects to make a profession in the field of education in the future.

  13. What do I intend to Learn next?

      What I intend to learn next is somewhat similar to a Liberal Arts Education. However, I will not have readily available professors to guide me- I will be seeking the resources and guidance myself. Keep in mind that the following are the result of Ad-hoc Interests and more Interests will pop out along the way.

    1. Human Nature and Psychology

        Having learnt introductory Psychology, I intend to delve a little into Social Psychology, possibly both from a Psychologist’s and a Sociologist’s viewpoint.

    2. Money Supply and the Capitalist Economy

        Knowing exactly how our currencies originated and how they work has always been a wish of mine. I will be learning by pressing toward this goal while learning the required theories as and when I need them. I intend to also constantly consult experts in the field and my friends who are more knowledgeable than me. Towards the end, I expect to have a foundation in macroeconomics while learning the necessary microeconomic theories along the way.

    3. Sales, Marketing, Business, Finance and Logistics

        I intend to get a overview on the content and knowledge gained by students in the above fields. I mainly want to be able to understand the basic lingo used in these fields and be able to strike a conversation about these topics. Also, I would be focusing on seeing the links between the above fields and the knowledge (e.g. in the sciences) that I have already gained thus far. It is likely that I will be mainly involved with introductory textbooks and students in these fields.

    4. Basic Law in Singapore

        Having realised the significance of the Constitution through my self-directed learning, I intend to commit some of its clauses to memory. I also intend to get an overview of Singapore Law in the fields that are of interest to me.

    5. Nutrition and the Half-an-hour meal

        I am considering stopping eating meats and seafood mass produced by the industry due to my suspicions about their feed, rearing methods and state of health when slaughtered. I intend to instead consume more from lower down the food-chain so as to minimize possible adverse health effects. Also, I want to investigate on my suspicions and learn more about vegetarianism while changing my diet slowly. Part of this will be to practice cooking meals for myself within half an hour.

    6. Exercise as a Daily Habit

        Currently, I know several exercises that can be done without going to a gym, that stretch and work out every major muscle group in the body. I intend to organise these exercises together into a form that can be done as a daily habit and assimilate them into my daily life. I also intend to learn more from experienced personal trainers and read up about the bio-mechanics and physiology behind these exercises.

    7. Inline Skating Proficiency and Instruction

        An example of the diverse range of topics I intend to cover, skating proficiency requires understanding of theory and methods and putting them into practice. I also intend to be trained as an instructor.

    8. Videography

        Videos play a huge role in the mass media. I intend to learn what it takes to film and edit videos of reasonable quality to add to the skills I have.

    9. Biographies of Select People

        I intend to first read general overviews of biographies of Martin Luther King, Mohandas Gandhi, Thomas Jefferson, Benjamin Franklin, Albert Einstein, etc. I will then be investigating further into areas of their lives that I find interesting and useful for me to know about.
