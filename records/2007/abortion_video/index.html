Abortion Video

  1. Inspiration

      I had received a forwarded email from Mr Leonard which had several pictures of aborted foetuses. That made me remember the documentary “The Silent Scream” that I had seen when I was much younger.

      Its speciality was in showing ultrasound videos of what happens in the womb during an abortion. I had seen other videos on abortion, but they all only showed images outside of the womb.

      Unfortunately, the quality of the ultrasound imaging back then was much lower than that of today’s. That made it difficult for lay people like me to discern what was actually happening inside the womb.

      So I thought, why not a remake of the Silent Scream with today’s ultrasound imagery technology.
