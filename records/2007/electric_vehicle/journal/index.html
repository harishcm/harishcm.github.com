        4. Initial Perception
          1. We can have electric cars today
          2. Electric cars will cause tremendous changes in the industry
            1. Internal combustion engine companies

                Electric engines do not require replacement parts, motor oil, filters, spark plugs, and require much less maintenance.

            2. Brake companies

                Due to regenerative breaking, a lot less brake wear is caused.

            3. Tyre companies
            4. Fuelpipe/silencer companies

                Electric cars have no direct emissions and have a lot less silencing requirements.

          3. Resistence to change in the industry
            1. Huge industry worth billions of dollars
            2. No point killing the current golden goose
          4. Consumer wants
            1. Big, loud, powerful cars
            2. Cars are a status symbol

              Expect to pay less for a totally sufficient but less powerful electric car.
