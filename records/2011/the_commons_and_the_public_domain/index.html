The Commons and the Public Domain

  1. Inspiration

      “Creativity and innovation always builds on the past. The past always tries to control the creativity that builds on it. Free societies enable the future by limiting the past. Ours is less and less a free society.” — Lawrence Lessig.

      This is an attempt at learning about the Commons and the Public Domain. I have been interested in this topic since at least 2008. I had benefited greatly from free, libre and open source software, and had gotten to know about Creative Commons. These days, my work requires me to publish academic articles. I feel it is a good time to compile resources related to this topic separately.
