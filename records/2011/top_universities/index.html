Top Universities

  1. Inspiration

      It would be greatly beneficial if I were to have international experience and exposure, of different forms, such as academic, professional, corporate, non-profit, philanthropic, social and cultural. From the academic aspect, society, or at least Indian-Southeast Asian-Chinese societies, put great weight on the university that one attended.

      Even if one attended the university way back in one’s late teens and early twenties, the weight it carries seems to last throughout one’s life, and even thereafter as well.

      From the little I know, it seems that several European, North American and South American societies are strikingly similar as well.

      From a Singapore perspective, the university that one attended is one of the most important piece of information that one is judged by, when it come to hiring and promotion in academia and in government and in multinational or government-linked companies.

      Personally, I oppose judging people largely based on the university they attended. Socio-economic and geographic factors can usually explain more about the university that one enters, than capability (assuming it can be gauged effectively). Further, I find judging an adult person based on what happened when he/she was in his/her late teens/early twenties to become increasingly irrelevant as the person being judged gets older, though I concede that a small number of issues that happened at that age would continue to be relevant throughout one’s life.

      On the other hand, I do understand that the university one attended is one of the heuristic measures that are used for making quick decisions and forming quick opinions about people. Yet, making it a large and everlasting part of one’s judgment of another is being lazy, at best.

      The above idea is one through which I am looking at the possibility of attending one of the “top” universities in the world. I am currently compiling a list of universities that are considered top according to different criteria and ranking schemes.
