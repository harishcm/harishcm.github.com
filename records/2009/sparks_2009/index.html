  17. 2009-05
    1. Question
      1. How long can spiders shoot a line of their webs?
        1. Inspiration

            Sadu, Kin Fei, Wei Xiong and I were observing a tiny spider at a coffee shop in Tampines after a failed attempt at star gazing with Wei Xiong’s telescope.

            We first observed the spider moving its jaws and front legs in a repeated motion. Then we realised that it was actually shooting a line of its web over a considerable distance as I felt it land on one side of my forehead.

            We were then shocked that it shot a line of its web over a distance of more than one metre. That interested us in playing around with cutting the last line it shot and observing it shoot another.

            After some time, the spider did its repeated jaw and leg movement again. Only this time we couldn’t locate the web line that it shot. Then suddenly it appeared as though it was flying. We realised that it had shot a line from our table all the way to the roof than was over 5m away. That left us in complete shock, awe, admiration and me with a burning question in mind.
