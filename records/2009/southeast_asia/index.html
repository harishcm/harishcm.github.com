Southeast Asia

  1. Inspiration

      It began ever since I was in the USA. I started to realise that Southeast Asia was extremely diverse—the nature, the multitude of peolpes and cultures, the so many ways of living… It was all around me, but yet, my travels around the region had been so far and few.

      Perhaps it was the enriching cultural experience that I had in the US that started me thinking this way—the great number of museums, the great number of authors, the diverse issues that they talked about, the different perspectives that I could receive, and so on.
