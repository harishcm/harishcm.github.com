  2. Inspiration

      The book was listed as a supplementary reading material for an NUS modules that I took (GE3210 Natural Resources: Policy and Practice). I realised that the author was Thai, and the book was about a study done in Thailand. That corresponded with my interests—wanting to know more about Southeast Asia, and wanting to read books from non-US authors. Of course, the themes of natural resources, forest peoples, minorities, etc. were interesting as well. So I borrowed it and spent a week reading several chapters.
