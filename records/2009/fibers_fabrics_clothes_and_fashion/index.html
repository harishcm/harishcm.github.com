Fibers, Fabrics, Clothes and Fashion

  1. Inspiration

      Several months ago I became interested in exploring issues relating to clothes and fashion. I had noticed that some of my t-shirts kept me less sweaty and more comfortable than others in Singapore’s tropical climate.

      On one of the comfortable t-shirts, the fabric had several visible tiny holes. It also had several vertical ‘lines’ on it, which probably made it easier for moisture to spread over a larger area and then evaporate away faster.

      I then came across an opinion that it was very difficult for synthethic fibers to emulate the breathability and other properties of natural fibers.
