Left Knee Therapy: Journal

  1. 2011-07-15
    1. Possible Therapy
      1. Focus solely on keeping the knee cap tight with the knee joint

          Controlling the actions of the entire leg while walking is not helpful as the movements of the entire leg are too complicated to be consciously orchestral for each and every step. Conscious control of the movement of the entire leg results in the leg muscles and supporting structure tensing up, making the step unnatural, immediately defeating the original purpose.

          I have noticed that knee guards pressing the knee cap against the knee joint have worked. Pressing my knee cap onto the knee joint has worked as well. I have had initial success with consciously focusing on tightening the knee cap onto the knee joint. It allows me to achieve a greater degree of relaxation within the rest of my leg. It is also less mentally draining. I should probably continue trying this therapy out to see its effectiveness over a longer period of time.
