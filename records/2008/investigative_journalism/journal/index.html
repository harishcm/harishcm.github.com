      1. Sicko

        3. Perception 2008-05-23

            It is scary and not at all helpful for recovery if you have to continuously be afraid about the medical bills when going to see the doctor for treatment especially when it is an emergency.

            Doctors too are distracted from their profession of providing care as they continuously have to worry about whether the patient can pay for the medical bills.

        4. Explore

          2. Cuba’s medical aid

              It was mentioned that Cuba was one of the leading doners of medical supplies and equipment to the third world countries. This goes in hand with Hans Rosling’s talk that the world has changed much in the past thirty years. I want to look into this too.
