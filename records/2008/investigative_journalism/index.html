Investigative Journalism

  1. Inspiration

      Learning how investigative journalists uncover data and events amidst danger and secrecy will improve my investigative skills. I also want to find out how research and investigation was done in the past before the advent of the internet and proliferation of telecommunications. I simply cannot imagine it. So, I want to find out.
