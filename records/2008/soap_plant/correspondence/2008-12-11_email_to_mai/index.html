Subject: Could you do me a favour regarding research on ``soap plants''?

Hello Mai,

I would like to ask you a favour. You see, I am doing some research on ``soap plants'' for this [World Toilet Organisation][wto]. We want to find plants that can be used directly as soap substitutes. The vision is to plant such ``soap plants'' in rural and urbans slums in Asia, so that access to soap is improved :)

[wto]: http://www.worldtoilet.org

Could you help me ask your parents/relatives if they have used or heard of such ``soap plants''? Also your grandparents or other elders too? I have found really good [database][pfaf] of such ``soap plants''. But they are a bit too Britain and America centered. Moreover, local and first hand information is definitely the best!

[pfaf]: http://www.pfaf.org/database/search_use.php?K[]=Soap

Thanks for your help!

Regards,
