Real Fast Food

  1. Inspiration

      I have been staying away from fast food restaurants for more than a year now, due to both the unhealthy cooking methods and my strong reservations against the way animals in their food supply chains are reared.

      There is nothing “faster” about fast food restaurants either. Neither waiting in the queue nor waiting for your food when it is not ready is fast. Ordering and receiving food from hawker centre stalls is just as fast, if not faster than fast food restaurants. Same or worse service, worse food and a “restaurant” just for namesake.

      Trying to be able to cook healthy and balanced meals within half an hour made me realise that there can be a lot of ways that one can prepare really healthy and balanced meals within minutes, often with no cooking. Maybe I should compete with there “fast food” outlets to show them and others that there is a better, faster and healthier way to get their food fast.
