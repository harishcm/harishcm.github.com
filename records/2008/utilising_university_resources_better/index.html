Utilizing University Resources Better

  1. Initial Inspiration

      I realised that the university is home to a huge wealth of resources and people who are extremely knowledgeable and skilled in their own fields. So I began to explore how I could try to make use of these resources and learn from the people.

  2. Current Inspiration

      I have completed a Bachelor’s Degree, and am currently working as a Research Engineer within the university itself. Much of the initial inspiration remains, but now, with an expanded and more flexible outlook. Now, the university has taken the role of a workplace as well. I am actively soul-searching with regards to what I wish to do with my life over the medium- and long-term. Considerations in utilising the university’s resources, such as in terms of qualifications, branding, life experiences, place or country of residence, learning about areas that I am less familiar, have become more of the norm. There is now also a greater issue of opportunity cost and a decision of how and whether to move out into the outside world for the experiences that it would provide.
