Subject: RE: Modules covering ``using local materials'' for building and construction

Dear Prof Ong,

Thanks for your reply.

My initial interest in contacting you was to find out more about using ``locally appropriate materials''. This was especially for construction during humanitarian crises and for development needs in rural and marginalised areas.

Some materials I have come across are adobe bricks, rammed earth, compressed earth, lime/cement stabilized earth, bamboo structure, rebar roof truss, etc. I wanted to find modules or other learning opportunities that would cover building techniques and preparation/sourcing issues for such building materials.

The research projects you shared sound interesting and in tune with my initial interest. Reusing materials from existing buildings relates to using materials which are already there locally.

I would be glad to meet up to find out more details about these research projects. I am available anytime on 26 or 30 December.

Best wishes for the year end festive season!

Regards,
Harish
