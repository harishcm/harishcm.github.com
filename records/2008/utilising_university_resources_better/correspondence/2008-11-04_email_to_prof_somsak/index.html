Subject: Initiatives that have emerged from Seminar with Dr Toh---Utility for the Rural Community?

Dear Prof Somsak,

This is Harish, from Year 2 Civil Engineering. Prof, ever since I attended the seminar on ``Utility for the Rural Community'', I have been interested in finding out more about Dr Toh and his company Grenzone Pte Ltd.

Prof, could I/the department be updated on initiatives have emerged from the discussion that all of us had in the Hibiscus room?
