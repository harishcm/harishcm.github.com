Milk Lactose Digestion

  1. Inspiration

      I watched this talkshow on Vijay TV in which doctors were commenting that drinking milk is likely to be detrimental to health. They said that after about 4 years of age, humans generally become lactose intolerant. They went on to say that this effectively means that milk cannot be digested by most adults. The undigested lactose is fermented in the large intestine by bacteria, resulting in large amounts of gas. This causes health problems, ranging from stomach bloating to diarrhoea.

      I had the notion, that milk was essential for a healthy balanced diet. What the doctors said was completely against that. I want to investigate further into the research findings that the doctors based their views on.

      I also want to explore the milk trade and the way it is and has been marketed to different cultures over the years.
