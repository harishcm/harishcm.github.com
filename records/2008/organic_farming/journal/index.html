  2. 2010-01-16
    1. Question

        Can tractors be used only when the ground is hard, e.g. during dry seasons?

      1. Perspective 2010-08-26

          Soil seems to be compacted more easily when moist than when dry. In the geotechnical engineering, soil is commonly compacted before roads or foundations are placed above them. There is an optimal moisture content at which the greatest density—best compaction—can be achieved. In practice, this usually means that water has to be added to soil to achieve the desired amount of compaction.

          In the case of farming, soil compaction is undesirable. Thus, it probably makes sense for tractors to only be used when the ground is dry.
