    3. Where do Singaporeans go, and at what time and day?
      1. Inspiration

          I think that going to the malls is a central hobby and pastime for many Singaporeans. I have envisoned going to the malls as a passive hobby that is extremely peer pressure driven. Maybe people’s central hobbies should be more ‘creative’ as in leading to the creation of stuff, tangible or intangible. Still, I feel that this aspect of Singaporeans lifestyle plays a central part in ‘making Singapore work’— economically, sociologically, (politically?), etc. I first want to explore who and how many people go to the malls and at what times and days.
