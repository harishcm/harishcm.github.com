Stable Marriage Problem

  1. Inspiration

      I was looking for what the acronym SMP meant in computing. Came across the ‘stable marriage problem’ in the Wikipedia disambiguation page for SMP. It was interesting that the Gale-Shapley algorithm was men(proposer)-optimal.

      Decided that it will be interesting to extend the algorithm to answer the question: Is it better to propose or to wait for others to propose to you, under various conditions/perspectives?

  2. Conditions
    1. Any man or woman can choose to be a proposer
      1. Does the algorithm still work?
      2. Is it still proposer-optimal?
      3. Must the ratio of proposers and proposees be equal?
    2. Different proportions of men and women are proposers
      1. What happens if everyone is a proposer?
      2. Benefits to both parties at different proposer proportions
  3. Perspectives
    1. How does the individual beat the system?
    2. Whole population optimisation
