I've been fine. Took 5 mods last sem, so was still manageable. But I didn't follow up on the issue much either. Yeah, I understand Uni life is such. But you guys have laid the groundwork :)

Yup I did send such a suggestion to LTA at this [webpage](http://www.lta.gov.sg/public_transport/pbs/pbs_info.htm).
It's a standard form. They did not get back to me though. Note that it's a suggestion form for a premium bus service.

I did ask for comments about such a service from a few of my friends who live in the east. Most were concerned about flexibility. They would often travel from NUS to elsewhere, say to give tuition, and only then back home. But I felt most would be appreciative of a bus that gets ~to~ NUS direct from their estates.

Hmm, since you are doing market research, you may want to check out Ngee Ann Poly's [free shuttle service][nyp] from Hougang, Clementi, Woodlands and Tampines interchanges during term time.
