Improving My Math

  1. Inspiration

      I feel that math is important in understanding theories across many disciplines. Knowing math is particularly important to pick out inappropriate and plainly wrong usage of math to give the impression that one’s theory or ideas are sophisticated.

      Math is also useful as a language, as a way of viewing things in a different perspective and in identifying properties that are unintuitive or were not obvious on the surface.

      Self-learning math is also another domain in improving my learning skills and resources, as is learning new languages.
