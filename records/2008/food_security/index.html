Food Security

  1. Inspiration

      I am greatly concerned that food security around the world is declining rapidly. Was first alerted to this problem by World Hunger 12 Myths. I have subsequently read numerous articles about massive food recalls and less than adequate / lenient enforcement actions.

      I also doubt the effectiveness of food safety certification testing. It appears to me to be a slow and tedious process that cannot keep up with the shear volumes and speed of food shipments. As usual there is a lot more that I should know about.

    3. Genetically Engineered Crops and Animals
      1. Rationale for creation and production
        1. Created mainly because they are patentable

            Other plants and lifestock are not patentable as they derive from methods of breeding that have been around for hundreds of years.

      2. Dangerous potential for curtailing biodiversity
        1. Culture of sharing, improving and preserving biodiversity

            It has been the culture and accumulated knowledge of farmers and animal rearers to grow a variety of species and strains of plants, share their knowledge and plant species with one another and preserve species with a large variety of resistance properties (eg. drought, specific diseases).

            This has long created a win-win situation for all farmers both in terms of being able to breed better species and reduce the chance of complete wipeout of their produce in times of disease or natural disaster.

        2. Incompatibility of such culture to monopolistic business plans

            The push for greater market share and monopolistic tendencies inhenerent in businesses such as patent proliferation will strongly discourage the sharing that has been present and beneficial for all so far.

      3. No way to measure risks involved
        1. It is unknown what possible future risks are

            There is no means for debate or calculation about risks as we do not even know what risks are present.

        2. Reluctance of Insurance companies to insure GMOs

            Insurance companies are unwilling to insure against long term harm caused by genetically modified organisms and products.

          1. Reason
            1. Cannot assign risk
      4. Contamination of non-GM sources through cross pollination
      5. Genetic modification is usually unsuccessful

          Most genetically modified organisms are physically and mentally disabled or have gentic diseases. Many of them also suffer from diseases arising from old age even when they are young.

    4. Ineffectiveness of Safety Nets
      1. Random food checks

          Only a very small percentage of food is checked for the presence of harmful substances. The overwhelming majority of food is passed unchecked.

  4. First Resolution
    1. Move down the Food Chain
      1. Inspiration
        1. The DDT story

            Levels of DDT were found to be more concentrated in animals higher up the food chain.

            Harmful chemicals can accumulate in the bodies of plants and animals over periods of time. When these plants and animals are eaten, the harmful chemicals get transfered to predator that ate them.

            Since a predator eats much more than its own body weight over its lifetime, the harmful chemicals tend to bio-accumulate inside the predator’s body.

            This leads to higher concentrations of harmful chemicals as one looks higher up the food chain.
