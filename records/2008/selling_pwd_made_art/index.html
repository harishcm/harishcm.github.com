Selling Art Made By People With Disabilities

  1. Inspiration

      We Are Family, book by 4 NTU students that exlpore the daily lives and issues that several people with disabilitites in Singapore face. Also Jack Sim, founder of the World Toilet Organization. He questioned why artwork by PWDs should not be sold at very high prices? As an example pitch “They see the world in a way that we cannot. Only they can come up with such a creation.” I wish to explore this possibility.
