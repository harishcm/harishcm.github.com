Civil Engineering: Journal

  1. 2012-06-22
    1. Added Possible Resources for Geotechnical Engineering
      1. Soil Mechanics in Engineering Practice
        1. BY Karl Terzaghi, Ralph B. Peck and Gholamreza Mesri
        2. Textbook, 1996, 3rd Edition
        3. Perception 2012-06-22

            It seems to be the classic book for soil mechanics in saturated soils. Explains the properties of soils clearly and has solutions to real-life problems.

      2. Soil Mechanics for Unsaturated Soils
        1. BY D. G. Fredlund and H. Rahardjo
        2. Textbook, 1993
        3. Perception 2012-06-22

            Seems like it can possibly be taken as a companion book to “Soil Mechanics in Engineering Practice” for unsaturated soils.

      3. Earth Manual
        1. BY United States Bureau of Reclamation
        2. Manual, 1998
        3. Perception 2012-06-22

            Practical manual for earthworks and soil investigation.
