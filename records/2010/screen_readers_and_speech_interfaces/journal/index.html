    1. Experiment
      1. Using ORCA

          ORCA was nice but it still felt buggy and took too much overhead to perform its functions. A major problem was when I typed text quickly, and the speech engine could not keep up.

        1. Felt

            Perhaps I should look at the text and terminal based screenreaders instead. I have also read advice by a visually impaired person that text and terminal based screen readers worked much better.
